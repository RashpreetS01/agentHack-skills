# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in AgentHack-Skills a harassment-free experience for everyone, and to maintaining an environment where ethical hacking education is accessible, safe, and responsible.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Use respectful, inclusive language
- Focus on learning and education, not real-world exploitation
- Give constructive feedback on skills and lab environments
- Respect the ethical boundaries that keep this project safe and legal
- Report safety concerns promptly and privately

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Submitting skills or labs that enable attacks on unauthorized systems
- Sharing content that circumvents the educational/sandbox intent of any lab
- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile communication of any kind
- Requesting help using these materials for unauthorized or illegal activity

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for other behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
